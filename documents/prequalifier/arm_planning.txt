Title: Motion Planning for a Robot Arm

Brief intro of the project:
Robotic arms are now everywhere. However, planning their motion in cluttered environments remains a challenging problem due to the large number of degree-of-freedom (DoF). This project focus on the fundamental problem of motion planning for general robot arms.

Job description/workload
The student's work involves implementing motion planning algorithms for robot arms and conducting simulations on the MuJoCo platform and possibly real robot. The workload is expected to be at least 12 hours per week. 

Keywords of this project
Robotics, Motion Planning

Eligibility 
Students must finish the following prerequisite task and send the required material to zhongqiang.ren@sjtu.edu.cn to show their capability before joining the project.

Prerequisite: 
Your task is to read the paper and master the underlying concepts and methods (you may refer to the cited references for further information). Subsequently, you are expected to implement a recent motion planning algorithm based on the provided code base.
